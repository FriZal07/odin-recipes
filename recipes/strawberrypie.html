Strawberry Pie

Description

Strawberry Pie is a delightful dessert made with a buttery, flaky crust filled with fresh, juicy strawberries. The filling is often thickened with sugar and cornstarch, creating a sweet and slightly tangy flavor. Topped with whipped cream or served à la mode, it’s a refreshing treat perfect for spring and summer.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Pie crust
  • White sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • 3/4 Cup Water
  • Whipping cream

Steps

  1. Boil the strawberries
  2. Whisk cornstarch and water
  3. Pour the mixture in pastry shell
  4. Whip the cream
