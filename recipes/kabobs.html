Kabobs

Description

Kabobs are a popular dish consisting of skewered and grilled pieces of marinated meat, vegetables, and sometimes fruits. Originating from Middle Eastern cuisine, they offer a delightful combination of smoky flavors and tender textures. Kabobs are versatile, often customized with various seasonings and served with dips, rice, or bread.

Ingredients

  • Sauces
  • Seasoning
  • Meat
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Marinate the meat and vegetables
  3. Thred the ingredients onto skewers
  4. Grill the kabobs over medium-high heat
